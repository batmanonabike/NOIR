## **The City of Sandukar**  
Sandukar is a vast, authoritarian metropolis where corruption, fear, and moral decay define everyday life. The era resembles a distorted version of the 1940s: smoke-filled streets, flickering neon signs, trench coats, fedoras, and a constant sense of surveillance. Institutions are compromised, power is concentrated in faceless ministries, and hope is fragile.

### **Core Themes**
- **Corruption & Decay** — Institutions shaped by influence, bribery, and coercion.  
- **Oppression & Bureaucracy** — Rigid hierarchies, contradictory regulations, constant scrutiny.  
- **Violence & Desperation** — People act out of fear, survival, or pressure from those above them.  
- **Moral Ambiguity** — Choices rarely offer clean outcomes.  
- **Psychological Strain** — Trauma, guilt, addiction, obsession.  
- **Eldritch Undercurrents** — Forbidden knowledge, strange beliefs, unsettling phenomena.
